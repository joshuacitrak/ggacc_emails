Golden Gate Angling and Casting Club Membership Renewal
Greetings and Happy 2016, {Contact_First_Name}!

As a friendly reminder, please renew your Golden Gate Angling and Casting Club membership for 2016. Dues are per calendar year and must be renewed at the beginning of the year for your membership to remain current and participate in club events.

This year is shaping up to be fantastic year full of programming for you to enjoy the benefits of membership. January is starting off with a fly tying class, a rod building workshop and a long line competitive Spey casting workshop. A highlight for 2016 is the club's new 'Rondy' program which offers a broad range of fishing outings from almost no cost to fully guided backpacking trip. To date we have 19 Rondies on the GGACC calendar and expect to add another 6 for this year. In addition, if you're fairly new to fly fishing we continue to offer the Learn to Fly Fish Program and for the more experienced anglers there's the popular Spey Angler Program. Keep your eye on the club calendar and emails for more details and future events.

There is something for everyone at all levels of experience, so please renew for 2016 and I look forward to seeing you around the club. To renew you can simply log into your GGACC account and pay online, or you can contact me for instructions to send a check by mail if that is your preference. Should you ever have membership related inquiries, never hesitate to contact me and ask. I can be reached at Membership@GGACC.org.

Tight Lines!
Steve Hegedus
GGACC Membership Chair
